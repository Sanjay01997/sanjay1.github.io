This photo of Conservatory Pond in Central Park in New York City was taken on October 22, 2011 with a Canon EOS 30D camera.A 45-year-old woman has been hospitalised after a Molotov cocktail was thrown at her home in Sydney’s north. She was sitting on the front verandah of the home on Harkeith St in Mona Vale on Sunday night when the incendiary bomb was thrown just before 10.30pm. A fire “broke out on the verandah” on impact, but the woman quickly extinguished it with a garden hose before calling emergency services, NSW Police said. Paramedics treated her for minor burns on her arms and transported her to Royal North Shore Hospital in a stable condition, NSW Ambulance and NSW Police said.
